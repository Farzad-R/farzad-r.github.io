My name is Farzad Roozitalab, and I am an AI enthusiast.

About Me

I am a passionate AI researcher and a PhD student at Carleton University's Department of Mechanical Engineering, where I am part of the APEX team.
Following my passion to engage with AI enthusiasts, I am also the workshop lead at Carleton AI Society (CAIS), where I actively promote AI education and collaboration. In addition, I also started a weekly journal club, named "AI RoundTable", which is focused on cutting-edge advancements in AI. At our meetings, we discuss recent papers, and their associated code, and engage in lively debates on the implications of these findings. The main goal is to stay up-to-date with the latest research and industrial challenges and share knowledge with my peers, helping to create a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about AI and its potential. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in joining the club.
Beyond work, I enjoy exploring nature and watching movies. For a decade, I pursued my passion for extreme sports as a professional canyoner and spelunker. Today, I continue to seek adventure, whether it be through travel or tackling new research challenges.

AI-RoundTable

In AI Roundtable we bring together a community of AI enthusiasts to discuss the latest advancements in the field. Each week, we gather to review and analyze new research papers, sharing our insights and perspectives on the most cutting-edge developments in AI. Our discussions are centered around real-world applications and how these new ideas can be implemented in practical settings. In addition to our discussions, we also provide access to associated code, enabling our viewers to gain a deeper understanding of the algorithms and techniques we explore. Join us on this journey of discovery and exploration, as we push the boundaries of AI together.
